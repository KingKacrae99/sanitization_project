About the System

The FuLafia Management System is designed to enhance the cleanliness and hygiene of the university environment. It allows students, staff, and visitors to report sanitation issues such as littering, broken facilities, or any other concerns related to cleanliness. The system aims to promote a healthy and safe campus for everyone.

By using this system, you can contribute to maintaining a clean and pleasant environment at FuLafia. Your reports will be directed to the appropriate authorities for prompt action.
